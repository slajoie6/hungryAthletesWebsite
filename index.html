Hungry Athletes

Food is a human right and is a tangible and immediate way to help someone. It is our goal of the Hungry Athletes organization to make sure that NO PERSON goes without food and has the fuel they need to pursue both athletics and academics.

Hungry Athletes provides food to those in need. If you or someone is in need of food in the Garland, Texas area please click the apple on the right to apply. No personal information is required other than what we need to get the food to you through either delivery or pickup

We proudly work with 5 Loaves Pantry to provide food. If you would like to volunteer you can reach us at (469)668-6413.
